Orthographic projection

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Notice that:

  • There are two renderings of the same model: one at the origin; the other at (0,0,-8).
  • Parallel lines stay parallel.
  • There is no perception of depth. The "back object" looks as big as the center object and moving it or zooming the camera does not change the models relative size.
