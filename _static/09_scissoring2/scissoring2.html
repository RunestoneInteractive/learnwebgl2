Experiment with a Scissor Overlay

Drag the subarea to change its location.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
Manipulate parameters of createPerspective(fovy, aspect, near, far):

fovy (field-of-view y) : 45 degrees 5.0 179
aspect ratio : 1.0 0.1 5.0
near : 2.0 0.1 10.0
far : 10.0 2.0 20.0
